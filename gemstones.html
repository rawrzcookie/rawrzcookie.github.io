Getting Started

About This Tool

This Tap Titans 2 Gemstone Tool helps players compare current and new gemstones to determine which gemstone is stronger

How to Use the Gemstone Optimizer

  1. Select Damage/Gold Type - Choose your damage and gold type.
  2. Enter Current Gemstones - Input your current gemstone levels, bonuses, and values in the "Current" column.
    Tip: You can also paste your player export and automatically fill in your current gemstones using "Import Data" in the "Tools" section.
  3. Configure New Gemstones - Input the level and bonuses of new gemstonesin the "New" column to test potential upgrades.
  4. Compare Totals - The tool automatically calculates and highlights which gemstone is stronger (green border = better).
  5. Lock Important Rows - Use the lock icon to keep specific bonuses from changing when testing new configurations.
  6. Take New - When you find a better setup, click "Take New" to apply it to your current configuration.

Tips

  • Lock rows you don't want to change while experimenting
  • Use "Reset Current" or "Reset New" to clear all current bonuses and start fresh

Basic Gemstone Guide

Gemstones provide additional sources of damage and are unlocked either after reaching stage 150,000 or after 60 days played, whichever comes first.

Rarities

There are 5 different rarities of gemstones:

  • Common
  • Uncommon
  • Rare
  • Legendary
  • Mythic
Common and Uncommon will only have one row of bonuses.
Rare will have two rows of bonuses.
Legendary will have three rows of bonuses.
Mythic will have four rows of bonuses.
Higher tier rarities will appear and become more likely as you increase your Gemstone Level.

Priorities

The main factor that influences the effects of a gemstone's bonus is Level. The higher the gemstone's level, the stronger the bonuses.
Prioritizing Gemstone Level in the Mystic Research tree not only helps with stronger bonuses, but will also increase your chances of having higher rarity gemstones, which have more rows of bonuses
