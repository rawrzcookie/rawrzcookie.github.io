Skill Tree Builder

This tool helps distribute your specified skill points for highest damage efficiency.

Basic instructions:

  1. Enter your stats
  2. Enter the desired levels for skills you want your build to have
  3. Click on 'Optimize' to efficiently distribute your remaining skill points

Skill levels in the tree can be directly edited.

Auto QoL are hard-coded suggestions that differ based on SP ranges. If you prefer a different value, toggle the skill off and enter your desired value, or enter a higher value.

Toggle Skills enables selecting or deselecting of skills when optimizing. Toggle whether a skill should be included in the optimization. Disabled skills will be ignored.

Lock Skills enables locking or unlocking of skills. Locking a skill prevents it from being reset when you reset the tree, but it does NOT keep the skill at a fixed level when optimizing.

Quality of Life (QoL) skills will be ignored when optimizing as they do not directly increase your damage.

These can include, but are not limited to:

  • Titan skip and Stage skip skills
  • Cooldown Reducing skills
  • Mana skills
  • Probability or chance increasing skills
